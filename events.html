Game of Code 3.0

Game of Code 3.0, conducted under BVEST by MLSAC, provided a rigorous platform to sharpen logical reasoning and programming skills. Its two-round format:- a campus-wide Scavenger Hunt followed by a timed Puzzle Rush (challenged participants to apply analytical and teamwork abilities under pressure). With 74 teams across all academic years and a prize pool of ₹10,000, the event showcased strong student engagement and technical excellence.

UXplore

UXplore brought an energetic, design-focused atmosphere to the BVCOE Library on 19 September 2025, offering students a dynamic, time-bound platform to apply design-thinking skills. With 39 teams and 115 participants, the event's strategic bidding, problem-solving, and pitching rounds drove high engagement and earned strong feedback for its impactful structure and well-executed learning experience.

BugSmash

BugSmash, held on February 11, 2025, brought together 38 student teams to tackle debugging and code optimization. Its two-part format—error analysis on paper followed by a coding optimization challenge—tested logical reasoning, problem-solving, and performance-driven coding. The event sharpened essential skills for real-world programming under pressure.

TRP (tech, rumble and panchayat)

TRP (Tech, Rumble & Panchayat), held on 18th October 2024, brought together 29 teams to showcase critical thinking, technical expertise, and communication skills. After three rounds of public speaking, domain knowledge, and quick thinking, finalists faced a cross-questioning round that tested their ability to defend viewpoints and respond persuasively under pressure.

CreatiCon

CreatiCon, held on 18th September 2024, brought together 31 teams in a dynamic blend of creativity and strategy. The event strengthened critical thinking, design innovation, decision-making, and teamwork through its engaging auction and logo design rounds. It offered participants a rich platform to sharpen creative, technical, and presentation skills essential for modern problem-solving.
