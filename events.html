Game of Code 3.0


Game of Code 3.0, conducted under BVEST by MLSAC in collaboration with DSC Campus Blocks, offered a rigorous platform for enhancing logical reasoning and programming competence. The two-round structure—comprising a campus-wide Scavenger Hunt followed by a timed Puzzle Rush—enabled participants to apply analytical and collaborative skills under pressure. With participation from 74 teams across all academic years and a prize pool of ₹10,000, the event reflected strong student engagement and a commitment to academic and technical excellence.

UXplore


UXplore brought an energetic, design-focused atmosphere to the BVCOE Library on 19 September 2025, offering students a dynamic, time-bound platform to apply design-thinking skills. With 39 teams and 115 participants, the event's strategic bidding, problem-solving, and pitching rounds drove high engagement and earned strong feedback for its impactful structure and well-executed learning experience.

BugSmash


BugSmash was a premier event, held on February 11, 2025, where 38 student teams competed to master debugging and code optimization. The innovative two-part structure, featuring a pen-and-paper error analysis round and a high-stakes coding optimization challenge, pushed participants to their limits. This intensive experience successfully enhanced logical reasoning, critical problem-solving, and the ability to craft clean, performance-driven solutions, equipping students with essential skills for real-world programming.

TRP( tech, rumble and panchayat)


TRP( tech, rumble and panchayat) an event to foster critical thinking technical knowledge and effective communication skills among the participants was held on 18th October 2024. 29 teams enthusiastically participated in the event. The teams with best public speaking abilities, technical expertise in their domain and quick thinking ability made their way through the first 3 rounds to a final cross questioning round that challenged them to defend their viewpoints, enhancing their ability to think and respond persuasively under pressure .

CreatiCon


CreatiCon, held on 18th September 2024, brought together 31 teams in a dynamic blend of creativity and strategy. The event strengthened critical thinking, design innovation, decision-making, and teamwork through its engaging auction and logo design rounds. It offered participants a rich platform to sharpen creative, technical, and presentation skills essential for modern problem-solving.
